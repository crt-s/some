About the Game

Color Fill 3D is an innovative puzzle game that challenges players to strategically color three-dimensional spaces. With its intuitive mechanics and progressively complex levels, the game offers a unique visual and mental challenge.

Players navigate through increasingly intricate 3D environments, using color as their primary tool to fill, transform, and conquer each unique puzzle. The game tests spatial reasoning, strategic thinking, and color perception.

Each level presents a new geometric landscape waiting to be painted, offering endless hours of engaging and thought-provoking gameplay.
